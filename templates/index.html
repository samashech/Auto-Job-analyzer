Everything About RAIoT

RAIoT helps job seekers move from resume to real opportunities by combining resume understanding, skill extraction, and live scraping across multiple job platforms. Instead of spending hours searching manually, users get a focused stream of jobs that map to their profile and role preferences.

The platform is designed for practical use: upload or build your resume, let RAIoT analyze your profile, and receive jobs ranked by relevance. This creates a workflow that is faster than manual search and more personalized than generic job-board recommendations.

RAIoT is not just a list of links. It is a decision support layer for your career search, helping you prioritize where to apply first and where your profile has the strongest potential fit.

Core Capabilities

  • •Resume parsing from uploaded files
  • •Automatic skill extraction and profile enrichment
  • •Live scraping from multiple job portals
  • •Relevance scoring and dashboard ranking
  • •Saved jobs and application tracking
  • •Secure account system with password recovery
  • •Progressive job feed while scraping continues

Why It Matters

Many applicants miss opportunities because searching is repetitive and noisy. RAIoT shortens that cycle by surfacing matching roles, keeping your opportunities organized, and reducing the friction between discovering a role and applying to it.

By aligning jobs to your extracted skills and preferred role type, RAIoT reduces random searching and helps you focus your effort where outcomes are most likely.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about RAIoT.

How do I start?

Click Get Started in the top navigation, create your account, and upload or build your resume.

Does RAIoT apply jobs for me?

No. It finds and ranks jobs, then opens the source job page so you can apply yourself.

Can I save jobs?

Yes. Use the star icon on any job card to save it for later review.

Why do jobs load gradually?

Scraping runs in the background so you can see progress and results without waiting for the entire search to finish.
